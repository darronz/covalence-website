Covalence gives any AI client persistent, semantic, long-term memory. It runs locally on your Mac — your data never leaves your device.

## Quick Start

1. [Download Covalence](https://covalence.app/releases/Covalence-latest.dmg) and move it to Applications
2. Follow the [Getting Started](/docs/getting-started/) guide to connect your AI client
3. Start chatting — your AI will remember everything
