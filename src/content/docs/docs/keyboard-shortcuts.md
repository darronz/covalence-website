## Global Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Cmd+Shift+Space` | Open quick capture panel |

The global shortcut works from any app. It opens the quick capture panel as a floating overlay without switching windows.

The shortcut is configurable — open **Settings** (`Cmd+,`) and change it under **General**.

## Quick Capture Panel

The quick capture panel is a floating Raycast-style overlay for saving thoughts without interrupting your workflow.

1. Press `Cmd+Shift+Space` from any app
2. Type your thought
3. Press `Enter` to save — the panel closes and the memory is stored
4. Press `Esc` to dismiss without saving

Captured memories are stored immediately and appear in the Covalence memory list with source `capture`.

## App Shortcuts

| Shortcut | Action |
|----------|--------|
| `Cmd+,` | Open Settings |
| `Cmd+W` | Close window |
| `Cmd+Q` | Quit Covalence |

Standard macOS shortcuts apply throughout the app.

## Configuring the Global Shortcut

1. Open Settings with `Cmd+,`
2. Navigate to **General**
3. Click the shortcut field next to **Quick Capture**
4. Press your preferred key combination

The shortcut is saved immediately.
