<objective>
Gate the "Blog" nav entry (desktop row + mobile drawer) and the Footer "RSS" link on `posts.length > 0`. When the content collection is empty, the two visible entry-points to `/posts/` hide; otherwise they render unchanged. Mirrors the Wave 4 landing-page band's existing pattern (`{latestPosts.length > 0 && <section>…}`).

Rationale: Phase 2.1 Wave 5 was approved with a caveat — the blog has no posts yet, so promoting it in Nav + Footer points users at a blank page. Hiding the visible entry-points until the first post ships keeps the surface promise truthful. Invisible metadata (`<link rel="alternate">` in Base.astro head and Starlight head injection) stays because it costs nothing and auto-populates on first-post publish.

Output: A ~10-line change across two files. Empty collection → no Blog link, no RSS link visible. First-post published → both reappear on next build automatically, no config toggle.
</objective>
