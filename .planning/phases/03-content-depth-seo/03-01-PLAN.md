```
User-agent: *
Allow: /

Sitemap: https://covalence.app/sitemap-index.xml
```

Rationale (per D-06 and D-07):
- `User-agent: *` + `Allow: /` — allow-all default. Explicitly NO blanket AI-crawler blocks (no GPTBot, Claude-Web, CCBot, or Google-Extended rules). The site's entire pitch is open public content about giving AI memory, so blocking AI crawlers would be philosophically inconsistent.
- `Disallow: /releases/*.dmg` — uses the RFC 9309 `*` wildcard (matches zero or more non-`/` characters). Matches `/releases/Covalence-1.3.5.dmg`, `/releases/Covalence-latest.dmg`, `/releases/Covalence-1.3.4.dmg`, `/releases/Covalence-1.3.2.dmg` — all verified DMG URLs today. Does NOT match `/releases/` (the HTML index page — we want it indexed). Do NOT substitute `/releases/Covalence-*.dmg` — the wildcard-suffix form is more robust if a future DMG is ever named differently.
- `Sitemap:` — literal absolute URL (crawlers do not resolve relative URLs in robots.txt directives per Pitfall L1). Site is pinned at `https://covalence.app` per `astro.config.mjs:5`.

Formatting:
- Exactly 4 lines of content + one trailing newline (so `wc -l` reports 4).
- No leading blank lines, no comments, no extra blank lines between directives.
- Line endings: LF only (no CRLF).
